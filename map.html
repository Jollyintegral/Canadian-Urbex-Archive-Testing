Privacy Policy

Overview

Canadian Urbex Archive is built to show and manage map spots while keeping privacy simple. This policy explains what the site stores, what it does not track, and how optional analytics work.

Local Cache

Spot data may be stored temporarily on your device for about 120 seconds. This short cache helps the map load faster and reduces repeated data requests. The cache is local to your browser and expires automatically.

Location Privacy

The site does not track your location in the background. If you use a locate button or browser location feature, your browser may ask for permission and the location is used only for that map action. We do not store a browsing trail of where you move on the map.

Account Data

If you sign in, the site may use basic account details such as your user ID, display name, email address, role, and profile details needed for account features. Guest access avoids account-based features, but some map data may still load normally.

Spot Data and Uploads

When authorized users add or edit spots, the submitted information can include the spot name, description, coordinates, class, access role, timestamps, and uploaded images. This information is stored so the map can display it to users with the right access level.

Optional Analytics

Analytics are optional. If enabled, anonymous usage information may be used to understand general site activity and improve performance. Analytics are not intended to identify you personally, and you can turn them off from Site Settings at any time.

No Tracking Promise

We do not sell your data, track your browsing behavior across other websites, or build advertising profiles. The privacy controls in Site Settings are meant to keep this clear and easy to change.

Data Access and Control

Your account settings and privacy preferences can be changed from the site controls where available. If data needs to be corrected or removed, you may contact me via Email (liamnova9@gmail.com) or Discord (@Jollyintegral) for assistance.
